3. Real-Time Decision Support System for
Small Businesses (Web Application)
Problem Statement
Design and develop a web-based Real-Time Decision Support System that assists small and
medium-sized businesses in making informed operational and strategic decisions using data
analytics. Many small businesses generate data daily through sales, customer interactions,
inventory usage, and financial transactions, but they lack the tools and expertise to analyze this
data effectively. As a result, decision-making often relies on intuition rather than data-driven
insights, leading to inefficiencies and missed opportunities.
The proposed system should allow businesses to input and integrate data from various sources
such as sales records, inventory databases, customer feedback, and transaction logs. Inputs include
structured and semi-structured data such as daily sales figures, product demand, stock levels, and
customer preferences. The system should process this data using analytical models to generate
insights such as sales trends, demand forecasting, and performance metrics.
The platform should present results through interactive dashboards, charts, and reports that help
business owners make decisions related to inventory management, pricing, marketing strategies,
and resource allocation. Outputs include predictive insights, alerts for low stock or declining sales,
and recommendations for improvement.
The system must be scalable, user-friendly, and capable of real-time updates. It should also ensure
data security and privacy. The final output is an intelligent decision-making tool that empowers
small businesses to compete effectively in the market by leveraging data-driven insights.
Key Objectives
 Enable data-driven decision-making
 Improve business efficiency
 Provide real-time insights
 Reduce operational risks
 Enhance profitability
